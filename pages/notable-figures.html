Olat Giggs The Enigmatic Roamer

[Image: Olat Giggs wandering the streets of Grovis]

Background

Olat Giggs is the enigmatic co-founder and CEO of OLAT! - the company that created the MetroBlock server family. Despite his high-ranking position in the company, Olat spends the majority of his time wandering the streets of Grovis, the custom dimension created in MetroBlock 5, seemingly doing nothing of importance.

His origins are shrouded in mystery. Some say he was once a brilliant software engineer who created the original server architecture. Others claim he was a player who became so immersed in the world he decided to live in it permanently. The truth remains unknown.

Stories from the Streets of Grovis

The Quest of the Forgotten Key

In early 2025, a player named "ShadowCraft" spent three weeks searching Grovis for Olat Giggs. After finally finding him sitting on a bench in the Obsidian Gardens, Olat simply handed him a rusted key and vanished. The player later discovered the key unlocked a hidden chest in the abandoned clock tower, revealing a rare enchanted book that granted the ability to see hidden NPCs.

First reported: March 2025

The Day the Rain Stopped

During a particularly intense thunderstorm in Grovis, a group of players sought shelter under a bridge. As they waited, Olat Giggs appeared, sat beside them, and began humming a tune. As he sang, the rain gradually stopped, and the clouds parted to reveal the stars above Grovis - a phenomenon never recorded before or since.

First reported: June 2025

The Whispering Walls

A player named "Echo" claimed that after following Olat for an entire day, they heard faint whispers coming from the walls of the abandoned library where Olat had been sitting. When they investigated, they found a hidden passage leading to a room filled with ancient scrolls detailing the original design documents for MetroBlock 1.

First reported: August 2025

Important Notes

While Olat Giggs is the most enigmatic figure in MetroBlock history, he is not the only one who has shaped the server's development:

  • None of the figures listed here have ever been officially confirmed by OLAT! as employees
  • Many stories about Olat Giggs are considered urban legends by the community
  • Some players believe Olat Giggs is an AI construct created to guide players through the server's hidden features
  • OLAT! has never commented on the existence or non-existence of Olat Giggs

Remember: In MetroBlock, sometimes the most important figures are those who are not seen.
