FAQ & Support

Answers to the most common MetroBlock questions, from launcher issues to update mismatches and general gameplay confusion.

What version do I need to join MetroBlock: Infinite?

You should use Minecraft 1.20.1 with Forge 47.4.20 unless the Downloads page says otherwise for the current live build.

Why does the server reject my connection?

The most common reasons are using the wrong Minecraft version, missing required files, or loading extra mods that conflict with the server setup.

Can I reuse another modpack instance?

It is strongly recommended to keep MetroBlock in its own dedicated profile so other mods, configs, and keybinds do not cause avoidable issues.

Where do I report bugs or ask for help?

The best first stop is the community Discord. Include your game version, loader version, and a short description of what you were doing when the issue happened.

Do old server versions still matter?

Yes. Older versions are part of MetroBlock history, and some lore, references, and community traditions carry forward into current seasons and content.

When Asking For Help

Clear reports are much easier to solve quickly. Try to include the exact version, the page or feature you were following, and whether the problem happens every time.

  • State your Minecraft and Forge versions
  • Describe what you expected versus what happened
  • Note whether the issue started after an update or file change
